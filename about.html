About the Museum Owner

The James Bond 007 Museum was established out of a deep passion for the filmography of Ian Fleming's legendary spy. Our founder, Gunnar Schäfer, who developed an early interest in Bond, has built an outstanding collection that embodies the excitement, glamour, and action of James Bond. Over the years, the museum has become a must-see for Bond fans from all over the world, with visitors stopping by to step into the shoes of their favorite secret agent. From original movie props to interactive exhibits, we offer a unique and immersive experience that pays tribute to the 007 franchise's extensive history.
